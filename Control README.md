# AI Governance Compliance Controls

This document provides a comprehensive mapping of the foundational AI governance controls supported by the platform, linked to various global frameworks.

## 1. NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF)

| Control ID | Control Description | Artifacts to Collect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **NIST-GOV-1** | Policies, processes, procedures, and practices across the organization related to the mapping, measuring, and managing of AI risks are in place, transparent, and implemented effectively. | AI Governance Policy, SOPs |
| **NIST-GOV-2** | Accountability structures are in place so that the appropriate teams and individuals are empowered, responsible, and trained for mapping, measuring, and managing AI risks. | Org Chart, RACI Matrix, Training Logs |
| **NIST-GOV-3** | Workforce diversity, equity, inclusion, and accessibility processes are prioritized. | DEIA Policy, Hiring Metrics |
| **NIST-GOV-4** | Organizational teams are committed to a culture that considers and communicates AI risk. | Risk Culture Survey, Communications Plan |
| **NIST-GOV-5** | Processes are in place for robust engagement with relevant AI actors. | Stakeholder Mapping, Engagement Logs |
| **NIST-GOV-6** | Policies and procedures are in place to address AI risks and benefits from third-party entities. | Vendor Risk Assessment, SLAs |
| **NIST-MAP-1** | Context is established and understood for the AI system. | System Context Document |
| **NIST-MAP-2** | Categorization of the AI system. | AI System Inventory, Taxonomy |
| **NIST-MAP-3** | AI capabilities, targeted usage, goals, and expected benefits and costs compared with appropriate benchmarks are understood. | Business Case, ROI Analysis |
| **NIST-MAP-4** | Risks and benefits are mapped for all AI actors. | Risk/Benefit Matrix |
| **NIST-MAP-5** | Impacts to individuals, groups, communities, organizations, and society are characterized. | Societal Impact Assessment |
| **NIST-MEAS-1** | Appropriate methods and metrics are identified and applied. | Metrics Dashboard, Validation Report |
| **NIST-MEAS-2** | AI systems are evaluated for trustworthy characteristics. | Technical Testing Results, Fairness Audit |
| **NIST-MEAS-3** | Mechanisms for tracking identified AI risks over time. | Key Risk Indicators (KRIs) Log |
| **NIST-MEAS-4** | Feedback about efficacy of measurement is gathered. | Continuous Monitoring Plan |
| **NIST-MAN-1** | AI risks based on assessments and other analytical methods are prioritized, responded to, and managed. | Risk Mitigation Plan |
| **NIST-MAN-2** | Strategies to maximize AI benefits and minimize negative impacts are prioritized. | Innovation Strategy Document |
| **NIST-MAN-3** | AI risks and benefits from third-party entities are managed. | TPRM Reports (Third-Party Risk Management) |
| **NIST-MAN-4** | Risk treatments, including response and recovery, and communication plans for the identified and measured AI risks are documented. | Incident Response Plan |

## 2. EU Artificial Intelligence Act (EU AI Act)

| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **EU-ART-12** | Record-keeping (logging) to ensure traceability of the AI system's functioning. | Audit Logs, System Logs |
| **EU-ART-13** | Transparency and provision of information to users. | User Manuals, Transparency Disclosures |

| **EU-ART-17** | Quality management system setup and compliance. | QMS Manual |
| **EU-ART-50** | Transparency obligations for providers and deployers of certain AI systems (GenAI, deepfakes, chatbots). | GenAI Watermarking Policy, Disclaimers |
